Our story began nine years ago on St. Patrick's Day in Washington, DC.
We met through mutual friends who were all going out to celebrate the holiday.
Throughout the night we got to know each other and it wasn't long before we knew there was a spark.
At the end of the night Jillian asked Oliver for his phone number. Or, depending on whom you ask, Oliver asked Jillian for her phone number.
It wasn’t until six years later that the luck of the Irish kicked in and that spark was rekindled.
We set off from Gravelly Point for an afternoon bike ride. The weather was chilly, overcast and at one point rained but all the while we rode all around D.C. laughing and catching up.
The sun was quickly setting but Oliver didn’t want to end the day so he asked Jillian out to dinner. Needless to say, a flame ensued.
Time flew by happily that day and it's been an amazing adventure ever since!
We’ve hiked the Nā Pali Coast of Kauai.
We've segwayed through the Windy City.
We've cruised the blue waters of the Caribbean.
We've paddled a remote island of Saint Martin.
We’ve explored the Blue Ridge Mountains.
But one adventure in particular has changed our lives. In November we vacationed in Cancun, Mexico with the plan of having fun in the sun! However, when we arrived the skies were cloudy and the weather forecast called for rainy mornings. Unfortunately, the weatherman was right. Each night Oliver checked the next day’s sunrise time and weather with hopes that we could see the sunrise on the beach at least once before we leave. Finally on our fourth morning, the skies were clear!
As we sat in the stillness of the morning, with no one on the beach but us, we shared stories about the past two years. Tomorrow would mark our two year anniversary. While we were reminiscing Oliver pulled out a small book titled “Our Story” that he made for Jillian, for this exact moment. We flipped through the book recounting memories paired with our favorite pictures. When we flipped to the last page Jillian read, “Will you marry me?” Jillian was completely surprised and overcome with happiness. Oliver had pulled off the best proposal (according to Jillian) And on that morning when the sun rose, Oliver knew Jillian was the one he would want to spend the rest of his life with.
Life is something that is meant to be experienced; the good, the bad, the triumphs and the hardships. Forever by each other’s side, we look forward to life’s next adventure.
